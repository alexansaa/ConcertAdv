CREATIVE COMMONS

GLOBAL SUMMIT 2015

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activist who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different contry every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organization and individuals who wnat to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free cultura and open knowledge.
So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge _ free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, gallerieas, libraries, museums, archives, goverments and fundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, mmore vibrant commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about CC Global Summit 2015!
